Green Roof Suitability

This interactive map visualizes the greening potential of rooftops in Gdańsk based on slope, height, shape ratio, and area.

  • Scenario 1: Buildings are filtered based on Height, Slope, Area, Surface Ratio with their respective thresholds.
  • Scenario 2: In scenario 2, apart from Scenario 1 factors, slope is further categorized into (0–5°, 5–10°, 10–15°, 15–20°) with lower slope = better.
  • Scenario 3: Same as Scenario 1 but excludes industrial buildings.

⚠️ This analysis is based on static building features and does not account for dynamic factors like structural integrity, climate impact, or ownership limitations.
